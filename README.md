#### Part I: Vector & Analytic Geometry
*   **Vector Space:** Free vectors, Linear independence.
*   **Products:**
    *   **Dot Product ($\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}$):** Angles, Orthogonality.
    *   **Cross Product ($\vec{a} \times \vec{b}$):** Normals, Area.
    *   **Triple Scalar Product $(\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c})$:** Volume, Coplanarity.
*   **Objects:** Equations of Lines (Parametric/Cartesian), Planes, Bundles.

#### Part II: Curves & Surfaces
*   **Conics:** The Ellipse, Hyperbola, Parabola.
*   **Quadrics:**
    *   Ellipsoid.
    *   Hyperboloid (of one sheet / two sheets).
    *   Hyperbolic/Elliptic Paraboloids (Saddle points).
*   **Generated:** Revolution surfaces, Conical/Cylindrical surfaces.

#### Part III: Computational Geometry (Transformations)
*   **Affine Transformations:** Translation, Scaling, Rotation, Shear, Reflection.
*   **Homogeneous Coordinates:** Representing $N$-dimensional points in $N+1$ dimensions to allow matrix multiplication for translations.
    *   Example: Point $(x, y) \rightarrow (x, y, 1)$.
